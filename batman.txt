Batman is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. The 
character was created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger, and first appeared in Detective 
Comics #27 (May 1939). Originally named "the Bat-Man", the character is also referred to by such 
epithets as "the Caped Crusader",[5] "the Dark Knight",[5] and "the World's Greatest Detective".[5]
